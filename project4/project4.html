Instructions


Welcome to Spot the Difference!
There are three levels in this game:Easy, Medium, and Hard.
When you hover over the image, it will change into another image image!
Now, spot the differences between the images!

Level: Easy

This level is pretty easy
You have 7 seconds to look at the image before hovering over it.
Now hover over it to see the next image.. 7.. 6.. 5.. 4.. 3.. 2.. 1..
Spot the difference!

Level: Medium

This level is a bit harder than the first one
You have 5 seconds to look at the image before hovering over it.
Now hover over it to see the next image.. 5.. 4.. 3.. 2.. 1..
Spot the difference!
